Welcome to Algeble2 Liar, at the beginning of the game, the machine will randomly generate an algebraic expression with an integral result from 100 to 999. The result is given to you in advance. Your goal is to deduce the algebraic expression of the expression with minimum amount of clues and guesses, which you get every turn.

During a turn, you are required to ENTER an algebraic expression that must result in a number randomly generated by the machine, different from its. Please note that the number generated by the computer has no relation with the required guess at all. The machine then compares your expression with its and gives you a “Liar Wordle format” clue (See below for details). You then use the clues to help deduce the machine’s original algebraic expression.

#please note that leading zeroes and formula redundacy such as 0+*/, +-*0, 1*, */1 are not allowed in clues.

Wordle format clues: A green tile means that the character is in the correct spot of the expression, a yellow tile means that the character is in the expression but in a wrong spot, a grey tile means the character, or extras of the character, is not included in the expression. In addition, exactly one tile, which you need to deduce, will give an incorrect color.
